Creator

Zoi Marmara

Zoi comes from web design and motion graphics, and her broader work lives under Anchored — a space exploring presence, emotional grounding, and human connection. Toi Zoi emerged from the tension between wanting to stay private and the necessity of showing up online.

The Manifesto:

If you are not visible on social media, it becomes easier to go unseen.

And as time moves on, meeting new people doesn’t always happen the way it used to.

So Toi Zoi exists as a small, playful answer to that.

If the person you’re looking for doesn’t find you, maybe companionship can still find its way to you.

There is a Toi Zoi for you.

For many in the LGBTQ+ community, this feeling can be even more pronounced.

So Toi Zoi reshapes it — into camp, colour, and a form of symbolic friendship.

🎈 “The first doll wears pyjamas because the idea came to me in bed — she wears my favourite ones.”
